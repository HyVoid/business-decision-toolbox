# BUSINESS DECISION TOOLBOX

A collection of lightweight business analysis systems
built with Excel & Google Sheets.

These tools do not replace ERP systems,
BI platforms, or enterprise software.

They productize proven business analysis methods,
allowing decision makers to transform operational data
into structured business decisions.

---

## Find the Area That Best Matches Your Situation

Every business faces different challenges, but most operational decisions fall into a handful of recurring areas.

If any of the situations below sound familiar, start with the corresponding catalog. Each catalog organizes decision-support tools around common business questions, practical analysis methods, and reusable solutions.

| If this sounds familiar... | Explore |
|---|---|
| **Revenue is growing, but profit isn't.** Cash is always tight, margins are unclear, or different reports tell different stories. | [💰 Profitability & Financial Decisions](catalog/profitability.md) |
| **Inventory keeps increasing while products still run out of stock.** Working capital is tied up, replenishment decisions are inconsistent, or warehouse visibility is limited. | [📦 Inventory & Supply Chain Decisions](catalog/inventory.md) |
| **Marketing budgets continue to grow, but it's difficult to explain what actually drives revenue and profit.** | [📈 Marketing & Growth Decisions](catalog/marketing.md) |
| **Teams are busy every day, yet operational performance doesn't improve.** Processes are inconsistent, manual work keeps increasing, or resources are difficult to coordinate. | [⚙️ Operations & Resource Decisions](catalog/operations.md) |
| **Compliance requirements are becoming more complex.** Regulatory changes, tax obligations, audit preparation, or governance activities consume increasing amounts of time and effort. | [📋 Compliance & Risk Decisions](catalog/compliance.md) |
| **Engineering standards exist, but applying them consistently requires repetitive calculations, manual interpretation, or engineering judgment.** | [🏗️ Engineering & Technical Decisions](catalog/engineering.md) |
| **Business data exists everywhere, but turning it into reliable decisions remains difficult.** Reports are fragmented, KPIs lack consistency, or decision-makers cannot see the complete picture. | [🧩 Data Architecture & Reporting Systems](catalog/data-architecture.md) |

## Business Decision Landscape

The toolbox is organized around seven recurring categories of business decisions. Rather than grouping tools by software features or technologies, each catalog focuses on a specific decision domain and the business problems commonly encountered within it.

| Business Decision Area | Typical Focus | Catalog |
|---|---|---|
| 💰 **Profitability & Financial Decisions** | Profitability analysis, cash flow management, budgeting, investment evaluation, cost allocation, and financial scenario planning. | [Open Catalog](catalog/profitability.md) |
| 📦 **Inventory & Supply Chain Decisions** | Inventory planning, replenishment, warehouse optimization, SKU analysis, supply chain visibility, and working capital management. | [Open Catalog](catalog/inventory.md) |
| 📈 **Marketing & Growth Decisions** | Marketing ROI, campaign performance, customer acquisition, growth analysis, attribution, and budget allocation. | [Open Catalog](catalog/marketing.md) |
| ⚙️ **Operations & Resource Decisions** | Operational efficiency, project management, resource allocation, workflow standardization, KPI management, and business operations. | [Open Catalog](catalog/operations.md) |
| 📋 **Compliance & Risk Decisions** | Tax compliance, regulatory requirements, audit preparation, governance, and operational risk management. | [Open Catalog](catalog/compliance.md) |
| 🏗️ **Engineering & Technical Decisions** | Engineering calculations, technical standards, design validation, industrial analysis, and engineering decision support. | [Open Catalog](catalog/engineering.md) |
| 🧩 **Data Architecture & Reporting Systems** | Reporting frameworks, KPI systems, data modeling, executive dashboards, and decision-ready information architecture. | [Open Catalog](catalog/data-architecture.md) |
