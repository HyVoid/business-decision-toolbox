# Marketing & Growth Decisions

> Marketing spend increases, but nobody knows what actually generates profit.

## Are you experiencing any of these situations?

- Campaign reports show activity but not economic contribution.
- Channels are judged by clicks or leads instead of profitable outcomes.
- Budget is allocated by habit rather than marginal return.
- CRM and paid media data do not connect cleanly.
- Agency reporting does not answer what to change next month.

## Typical business questions

- Which marketing activities actually generate profit?
- Which channels deserve more budget?
- Which campaigns create customers rather than just traffic?
- Where is the funnel leaking?
- Which marketing reports can be trusted for budget decisions?
