# Operations & Resource Decisions

> Teams work harder every year but operational performance does not improve.

## Are you experiencing any of these situations?

- Teams are busy but bottlenecks keep moving.
- Operational KPIs exist but do not change decisions.
- Service work is difficult to schedule, price, or audit.
- Projects lose margin through time and cost drift.
- Managers need a control surface, not another raw spreadsheet.

## Typical business questions

- Why are operations becoming inefficient?
- Where is time, labor, or cost being wasted?
- Which jobs, projects, or services need intervention?
- What should managers review every week?
- Why do we hit KPIs but still miss business goals?
