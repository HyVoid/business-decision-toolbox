# Profitability & Financial Decisions

> Revenue is growing, but profit and cash flow are not.

## Are you experiencing any of these situations?

- Sales increase but net profit does not improve.
- Cash is tight even when invoices and revenue look healthy.
- Pricing decisions are made without contribution margin clarity.
- Projects, customers, or locations look successful until shared costs are included.
- Investment choices are discussed without a simple scenario model.

## Typical business questions

- Are we actually profitable?
- Why is cash always tight?
- Which customer, product, or project creates the most margin pressure?
- How much room do we have before a downside scenario becomes dangerous?
- Which investment or acquisition assumptions drive the decision?
