# Inventory & Supply Chain Decisions

> Inventory keeps growing while operational efficiency declines.

## Are you experiencing any of these situations?

- Inventory keeps increasing.
- Cash flow keeps shrinking.
- Some products never sell.
- Other products constantly stock out.
- Nobody knows how much to reorder.
- Multiple warehouses cannot be coordinated.

## Typical business questions

- Which products trap the most cash?
- What should we replenish?
- Which SKUs should be discontinued?
- How much inventory is actually healthy?
- Where are supply chain bottlenecks?
