# Engineering & Technical Decisions

> Engineering standards exist, but they are difficult to operationalize.

## Are you experiencing any of these situations?

- Technical standards are known but hard to apply consistently.
- Engineering calculations are repeated manually.
- Estimating decisions depend on scattered assumptions.
- Teams need a practical decision model for technical work.
- Simulation or constraint logic must be visible to non-specialists.

## Typical business questions

- How do we operationalize technical standards?
- Which calculation governs this design or estimate?
- What assumptions drive the technical decision?
- Which constraint is binding?
- What should be reviewed before committing cost or design resources?
